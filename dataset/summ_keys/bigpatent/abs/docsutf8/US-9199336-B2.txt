the invention relates to a method for allocation of a pipe or several pipes with several pipe parts to be cut for a laser cutting installation , wherein the pipe parts to be cut are selected from an order table . methods include measuring a length of each of the one or more pipes , calculating , before measuring , a nesting of a pipe part relative to the same pipe part or a different pipe part of the order table , calculating a plurality of pipe allocation variants for a particular pipe of the one or more pipes with the pipe parts to be cut based on the nesting and the measured pipe length of the particular pipe , selecting one of the plurality of calculated pipe allocation variants as a desired pipe allocation , and initiating cutting of the particular pipe based on the desired pipe allocation .
