disclosed is a full dental tray for use in applying a fluoride gel simultaneously to both the upper and lower teeth .
the tray is a disposable , laminated structure including a hydrophilic foam interior formed and heat bonded to a polyethylene foam substrate .
the tray has upper and lower portions which are hinged together and are contoured to properly accommodate the particular tooth structure of the posterior and anterior teeth , and the general geometry of both the upper and lower jaws .
