a tattoo needle case comprises a seat including a front section , a rear section including a receiving space , and a breaking section interconnected the rear and front sections ; a plurality of needles disposed in the receiving space ; and a sheath sleeved on the seat for fastening the needles .
bending and breaking the front section will expose pointed ends of the needles prior to use .
the invention can protect the stored needles , particularly the pointed ends thereof .
thus , the invention can completely and satisfactorily solve the safety problem of being accidentally pricked by the needles during use .
